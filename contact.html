What to Expect

How Colleen responds

Colleen reads every message personally and responds as thoughtfully as she can. You can expect to hear back within 3–5 business days during regular periods.

For client inquiries, a brief initial exchange by email will help clarify what you are looking for before scheduling anything. There is no obligation and no pressure to commit until you feel ready.

For speaking and podcast invitations, please include your show or event name, your audience, and your timeline. This makes it easier to give you a helpful and timely response.

There is no wrong way to reach out.

Whether you know exactly what you want or are still figuring it out — a simple email is always a fine place to start.
