New Here?

A simple path to start

If you are new to this work and not sure where to begin, here is a three-step starting point.

1

Download the Starter Bundle

Start with the free Pain Care Starter Bundle to get a clear overview of how pain works and what is possible.

2

Try one or two of the Free Bite-Sized Learning topics 

Choose one short movement practice and notice what you feel during and after. There is no expectation - just curiosity.

3

Consider what is next for you

When you are ready, see whether 1:1 support, DARE to Heal online pain care program or the à la carte online courses are right for you.
