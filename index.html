An Integrated Approach

Multiple lenses
One coherent path forward

Colleen’s work weaves together Osteopathic manual therapy, pain neuroscience education, therapeutic movement, and somatic awareness. Nothing in the body works in isolation - and neither do you.

  • Osteopathic Assessment & Manual Care

    A skilled, attentive evaluation that considers structure, function, and the full person — not just the site of pain.  Together, we get to the root of your symptoms.

  • Pain Neuroscience Education

    Evidence-based explanations that reduce fear and build confidence — making treatment more effective and recovery more sustainable.  Often a heavy topic, Colleen makes pain science light, interesting and even fun at times!

  • Movement & Nervous System Awareness

    Science-based, playful, intelligent approaches to restoring movement.  All informed by an understanding of applied neurology, pain science and how the nervous & immune systems shape pain and recovery.

  • Professional Learning & Mentorship

    Frameworks and practical tools for Osteopaths, physiotherapists, Athletic Therapists, bodyworkers and movement practitioners ready to deepen their clinical thinking.

WHy COLLEEN

Depth of care. Clarity of practice.

With more than 28 years of clinical practice and teaching across Osteopathy, pain care education, Pilates, yoga, nervous system regulation, applied neurology and somatic movement, Colleen brings a rare breadth of perspective to each encounter.

Her work bridges Osteopathy, contemporary pain science, somatics and movement.  These are disciplines that are rarely taught together, and almost never integrated with the clinical depth and warmth she brings. She meets each person with curiosity & care.  The more complicated & complex the story, the more invested she is!

People come to Colleen having already tried many approaches. They leave with a different relationship to their bodies, and the tools to keep moving forward with genuine hope.

Grounded in pain neuroscience

Colleen's practice is informed by current research, not just tradition. She helps people understand the biology of pain in ways that are accessible, empowering, and clinically useful.  You'll may even find yourself having fun!  She's found a way to make pain care interesting, accessible & playful.  Feeling better doesn't have to feel like a job.

You are genuinely seen

You are not your diagnosis nor a set of symptoms to manage. Colleen is interested in getting to know you as  a person. She takes time to understand your full history in the context of your life.  She communicates in plain, direct language and works with you to help you reach your goals.

Outcomes, not just treatment

The goal is lasting change: better movement, less fear, more capacity. Whether you're a patient or a practitioner, Colleen's work is oriented toward what actually holds.

A practitioner who keeps learning

Decades of experience and still deeply curious. Colleen brings an evolving, humble approach to her teaching and clinical work — because the field keeps growing, and so does she.
